A short tap on a key with a gray dot will produce it's character. But a long press will open a context menu, allowing you to drag your finger to the desired character and release.

On every layer, a long press on the space bar will yield the following context menu. ('NBSP' is a non-breaking space, 'NNBSP' is a narrow non-breaking space, 'ZWJ' is a zero width joining character and 'ZWNJ' is a zero width non-joining character.)
