A short tap on a key with a diagonal bar will produce it's character. But a long press will open a context menu, allowing you to drag your finger to the desired character and release.

On every layer, a long press on the space bar will yield the following context menu. ('≈' is a non-breaking space, 'nnbsp' is a narrow non-breaking space, 'zwj' is a zero width joiner and 'zwnj' is a zero width non-joiner.)
